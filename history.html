Company History

Arya Citra Mineconsult (ACM) is a dynamic management consulting and commodities trading firm founded by Jeffrey Mulyono and Putri Aryacitra. With over 30 years of experience in strategic advisory and commodities trading, Jeffrey brings deep industry expertise, while Putri contributes more than 16 years of excellence in human resource strategy development, organizational design, and corporate culture enhancement. Since its establishment in 2006, ACM has evolved from a specialized consulting firm into a trusted player in the coal trading industry, providing integrated solutions for businesses and communities. With a forwardthinking approach, ACM stays ahead of market trends, delivering innovative strategies in business management and energy commodities. As global markets shift, ACM remains committed to meeting clients evolving needs while championing sustainable development goals.

- 2006

ACM began its journey in 2006, offering strategic consulting services across energy and mining industries, specializing in business management, project development, and operational efficiency.

- 2023

In 2023, ACM strategically expanded into coal trading, leveraging opportunities in the growing energy and commodities sector. This transition allowed ACM to meet rising domestic and international demand while ensuring compliance with industry regulations. ACM's adaptability and market foresight continue to position the company as a key player in the energy sector.
